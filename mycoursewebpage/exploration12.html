Introduction

Amazon Web Services offers a broad set of global cloud-based products including compute, storage, databases, analytics, networking, mobile, developer tools, management tools, IoT, security and enterprise applications(MLOs 2-4). These services help organizations move faster, lower IT costs, and scale. AWS is trusted by the largest enterprises and the hottest start-ups to power a wide variety of workloads including: web and mobile applications, game development, data processing and warehousing, storage, archive, and many others.

 

AWS Core Services

As you go through the videos, do try to understand the following concepts:

  • Compute: EC2, Lambda, Lightsail, Elastic Beanstalk
  • Storage: S3, Glacier, Snowball, EFS
  • Databases: RDS, DynamoDB, Elasticache
  • Networking & Content Delivery: VPC, Route53, CloudFront
  • Security, Identity & Compliance: IAM, Certificate Manager, Inspector
